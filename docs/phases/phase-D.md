# Phase D — Carry & parts

## Goal

One carried item at a time. Storage closet. Ticket types that require fetching a part (toner, PSU, etc.) then hold-to-install at the desk.

## In scope

- Carry slot + pickup/drop
- Storage stockpile locations
- Multi-step ticket definitions (fetch → install)
- Reboot-only tickets still work

## Out of scope

- Full multi-room campus (Phase E can extend storage into a real room)
- Ordering new stock between days (Phase F)

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Player can carry one part
- [ ] At least two ticket types: reboot-only and needs-part
- [ ] Missing part blocks completion until fetched
