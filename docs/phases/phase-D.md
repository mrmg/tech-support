# Phase D — Carry & parts

**Unlock:** Phase B complete (may run before C). **Unlocked** after Phase C.  
**Does not require Phase E.**

## Goal

One carried item at a time. **Same-map storage closet** (cupboard zone on the office floor — not a separate room). Ticket types that require fetching a part (toner, PSU, etc.) then hold-to-install at the desk.

## In scope

- Carry slot + pickup/drop
- Storage **closet / cupboard** on the Phase A office map
- Multi-step ticket definitions (fetch → install)
- Reboot-only tickets still work

## Out of scope

- Separate rooms / server room / printer room (**Phase E**)
- Ordering new stock between days (**Phase F**)

## Storage vs rooms

| Phase | What |
|-------|------|
| **D** | Closet zone on the **same map** as the desks |
| **E** | **Separate rooms** and larger floor plans |

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Player can carry one part
- [ ] At least two ticket types: reboot-only and needs-part
- [ ] Missing part blocks completion until fetched from the closet
- [ ] Closet is reachable without a room transition
